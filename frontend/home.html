Cardiovascular Disease Prediction

Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) are a group of disorders of the heart and blood vessels, and they are the leading cause of death globally. Early detection and prevention can significantly reduce the risk. This ML pipeline helps in analyzing data, building models, and making predictions for cardiovascular diseases.

Common Risk Factors

  • High Blood Pressure (Hypertension)
  • High Cholesterol Levels
  • Obesity and Unhealthy Diet
  • Smoking and Alcohol Consumption
  • Lack of Physical Activity
  • Genetic Factors

How to Prevent Cardiovascular Diseases

Preventing cardiovascular diseases requires a combination of lifestyle changes and medical intervention. Here are some key prevention methods:

  • Maintain a healthy diet (low in saturated fats and cholesterol)
  • Regular physical exercise (at least 30 minutes daily)
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Monitor and manage blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Manage stress through meditation, yoga, or relaxation techniques

How Machine Learning Helps in Prediction

Machine learning models analyze patient data to predict cardiovascular disease risks. Some of the commonly used models include:

  • Logistic Regression
  • Decision Trees & Random Forest
  • Support Vector Machines (SVM)
  • Neural Networks & Deep Learning
  • Gradient Boosting Algorithms (XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost)
